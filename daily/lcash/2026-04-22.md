**Decisions Made:**

- Strict pick extraction rules: only fire on explicit patterns like "[team] team total over 3.5" or "[player] over 6.5 strikeouts" — vague mentions filtered out

- YouTube dynamic rendering blocks Playwright selectors for video lists — `yt-dlp` is the reliable path for captions
- Raw keyword matching on betting transcripts produces way too much noise; need strict co-occurrence of subject+prop+side+line within the same sentence/breath
- Hosts often reference each other's picks ("my pick is exactly what Brian said") — consensus signals across hosts in same episode are a real pattern to track
