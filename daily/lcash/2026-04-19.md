**Lessons Learned:**
- **Resolver sequential bottleneck:** The resolver processes sports sequentially. NRL has 7 stale dates with a slow NRL.com fallback scraper, which blocks ALL sports behind it (AFL, 485+ SSE leagues). This is the root cause for most unresolved picks.

- **`get_unresolved_dates()` only checks `game_date < today`** — today's games won't attempt resolution until tomorrow. This is by design.
- **Auto-discovered SSE configs** are created during `_sse_startup()` and ARE available in `SPORT_CONFIGS` by the time the resolver runs its second loop.

**Action Items:**
- Fix resolver sequential bottleneck: parallelize sport resolution or implement priority ordering so NRL doesn't block everything

- Test OpticOdds `/fixtures/results` endpoint for esports leagues to confirm resolution is possible
