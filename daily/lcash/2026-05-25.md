### Profit Calculation

```
Wager:  $25 flat per pick
Win:    $25 × (decimal_odds - 1)    →  e.g. 2.10 = +$27.50
Loss:   -$25
Push:   $0
Void:   excluded entirely
```

Odds source: `opening_odds` (the price at trigger time) → fallback `closing_odds` → fallback `1.9`.
