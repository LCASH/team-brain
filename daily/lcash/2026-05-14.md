### Session (09:43) [value-betting-scanner]

**Context:** Building an OpticOdds Pinnacle CLV (Closing Line Value) audit pipeline for the value-betting scanner — storing independent CLV data to validate the existing devigged-blend CLV calculations.

**Key Exchanges:**
- `/grader/odds` is a paid OpticOdds auto-grading endpoint (returns win/loss/push for a bet). Returns 403 on current key. **Not needed** — `server/resolver.py` already replicates the same logic with more control over edge cases (DD/TD, voids, etc.). Keeping the custom resolver avoids opaque grading regressions.
- OpticOdds `/fixtures/odds/historical` endpoint exposes `clv` (closing line value) and `olv` (opening line value) fields for every market entry — `{"price": -146, "points": 8.5}` format, raw Pinnacle American price.
- Cross-validation of 580 LAL@OKC picks showed **8.07pp mean absolute divergence** between our devigged-blend CLV and OpticOdds' raw Pinnacle CLV, with notable sign-flips on individual picks.

**Decisions Made:**
- **Use OpticOdds CLV as an independent audit signal, not a replacement** for the devigged-blend CLV. The value is in the *shape* of disagreement, not magnitude (Pinnacle-only raw vs multi-book devigged blend).
- Added 6 columns to `nba_tracked_picks`: `optic_close_pinnacle_price`, `optic_close_pinnacle_points`, `optic_clv_pct`, `optic_clv_direction`, `optic_clv_divergence`, `optic_lookup_at` (migration `034_optic_clv_columns.sql`).
- Fixture matching improved from strict substring to **nickname-based fuzzy matching** (last word of team name) — recovered 349→4 unmatched fixtures (99% recovery). Abbreviated formats like `ATL Hawks @ MIA Heat` were the main culprit.

**Lessons Learned:**
- `news_agent/pick_writer.py` is a **second writer** into `nba_tracked_picks` with its own column list and strip-retry logic — two writers, no shared schema. Architectural debt to address later with a single source-of-truth schema validator.
- **Counter-intuitive CLV signal at n=100:** When Pinnacle moves *with* our pick (>+2% OO CLV), win rate is **47%**. When Pinnacle is neutral or disagrees, win rate is **59%**. Two hypotheses: (1) late entry after value evaporated, (2) reverse sharp signal where Pinnacle reacted to wrong public action. Needs n>200+ to be conclusive.
- Match rate limited by: OO data retention (754 picks with 0 Pinnacle historical entries), alt-line mismatches (182 picks), and only 4 truly missing fixtures.
- Mean signed divergence is −4.55pp (we systematically under-estimate Pinnacle's move). Newly recovered abbreviated-name-format picks have larger gaps, possibly from older/sparser trail data.
