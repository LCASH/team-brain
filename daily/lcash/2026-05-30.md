## Fix 2: MLB 4-Book Power EV Cap — Safest Path

The agent found a **zero-code-change shortcut**: tracker.py already has a `max_ev_if_sharps_gte` filter (lines 1403-1408). We can abuse it with `sharps_gte: 1` (always true) to cap EV at 50%:

    weights,
