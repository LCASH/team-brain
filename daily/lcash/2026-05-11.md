### The Centralized JSON (what you cared about most)

`GET /v1/odds?sport=mlb` returns **every market with every book's quote side-by-side**:

- **One dict per market** — keyed by `market_key` (player + prop + side)
- **All books in `.books{}`** — Bet365 (365), FanDuel (100), DraftKings (200), Novig (192), Hard Rock (803), Kalshi, Polymarket, Bovada, etc.
- **Same schema everywhere** — `line`, `odds`, `captured_at` per book
- **Live updating** — NBA every 30s, MLB every 60s, sub-second WS deltas for in-play
